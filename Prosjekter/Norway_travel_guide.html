The People

Now for the people!

I personally think that Norway is a place filled with lots of nice and kind people. Some might comment on how we might be a little "to ourselves" and that I can understand, because some of us don't open up immediately like in some other countries. But let me tell you this, when you finally get to befriend some of them, they might just end up becoming some of your closest and best of friends and will be there for you when you need it. You might notice that I really love this country, I mean who wouldn't?

Norwegians are also known for their strong sense nationality and strong love for everything Norwegian. So what are you waiting for? Book a ticket to Norway and find out for yourself if what I tell you is true or not! ps: Don't worry too much about not understanding the language, Norwegians often have a decent English.

The Culture

We Norwegians of course love our country, that means that nature and outdoor activites are a good part of our culture, after all "Norwegians are born with skis on their feet". Winter sports are one of our specialties. We help our community. We don't think we are better than everyone else as individuals (But as a country we are definitely better than most. 100% True). Democracy and union are big things for us. We also love our cabins, huts or whatever you want to call them, just taking a trip up to your cabin and just living life on a vacation is just the best for some of us. We are also a very competitive, especially towards our other scandinavian countries, it's always a sad day seeing Denmark or Sweden (especially Sweden) above Norway in sports or other competitions, but then it's good that it's rare for Norway to be under them. Never cheer for Sweden or Denmark as a Norwegian, but Finland is ok, we like Finland. And one last that is that we are proud of our viking history, I mean vikings are super cool after all!
